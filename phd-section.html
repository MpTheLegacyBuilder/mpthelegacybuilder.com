Research Interests

AI Integration for Enterprise & Defense

Exploring how artificial intelligence can be operationalized within enterprise and DoD environments to enhance decision-making, operational resilience, and infrastructure agility.

🛡️

Zero Trust Architecture

Researching the implementation of Zero Trust frameworks across complex organizational environments, with emphasis on identity verification, micro-segmentation, and continuous validation.

Agile Modernization in IT

Examining how Agile methodologies drive IT modernization initiatives — particularly within legacy-heavy enterprise and government environments transitioning to cloud-first infrastructure.
