About LegacyPath

LegacyPath Estate Planning LLC was founded out of personal experience with the pain and confusion that follows when a loved one's affairs are not in order. We are a professional document preparation firm dedicated to helping individuals and families organize their estate with compassion, clarity, and confidence.

We specialize in drafting wills, powers of attorney, and advance directive documents — using state-compliant templates — while connecting clients with trusted legal professionals when needed. Our goal is simple: make estate planning accessible to everyone, at a price that doesn't require a retainer.
